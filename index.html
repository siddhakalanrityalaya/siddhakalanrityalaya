Meet the Guru

Mrs. Bhargavi Prashant Raibagi


साै. भार्गवी प्रशांत रायबागी

Mrs. Bhargavi Raibagi founded Siddhakala Nrityalaya in order to educate students about traditional Indian dance form Kathak.
She is a disciple of Pandit Vasantkumar Kulkarni and has successfully completed Nritya(Kathak) Visharad under his guidance. It has been 10 years since Siddhakala Nrityalaya started and has produced excellant dancers over the decade.
